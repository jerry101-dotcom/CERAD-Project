The Centre for Research and Development (CERAD) has been anchoring research, training and organising conferences in the Bamidele Olumilua University of Education, Science and Technology, located at Ikere-Ekiti, Nigeria.
Hence, this year International Conference on Education, Science and Technology (ICEST, 2022) has the theme: Dynamics of Global Turbulent Era: Building Resilience and Recovery.
The LOC of the ICEST 2022 is ready to accommodate all participants to share their experiences about the global turbulence, resilience and recovery.
