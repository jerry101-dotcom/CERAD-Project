ACADEMIC EVENTS

The Centre for Research and Development (CERAD) has its core objectives to promote research among the early and intermediate researchers to grow exponentially in publication including publishing in high impact journals and with reputable publishers. Among our activities include: training at workshops, seminars, research exhibitions, summit, colloquium, roundtable discussions, etc.

The Centre usually processes Institution Based Research (IBR) and National Research Fund (NRF) grant with Tertiary Education Trust Fund (TETFund). Thus, eighteen (18) academic staff of the University have enjoyed IBR from TETFund and there is one NRF research currently ongoing as approved by TETFund.
