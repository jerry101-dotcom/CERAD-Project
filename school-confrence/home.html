Special Information about the Conference

The conference events are subject to change considering the NCDC guidelines and global health safety concerns on COVID-19. Nevertheless, the conference is organized using blended presentations – physical and virtual. All physical attendants should come with their nose masks. Also, note that all official updates will be posted on the conference website and whatsApp conference platform as soon as they are available. Please, provide your WhatsApp number to receive quick messages.

EXPECTATION FROM PARTICIPANTS

Participants are expected to follow these Guidelines.

Article for plenary session presentations must include:

  1. A soft copy of the full paper which should be submitted on or before Thursday, 30th May 2024 through the Conference website. Please download the MS-Word document here to prepare the manuscript.
  2. A well prepared power point (slides) for presentation. This should be submitted on or before Friday, 14th June 2024.
  3. Poster presentation is welcomed. The author shall bring it to the hall for presentation
  4. Abstract and full paper should be submitted through the official conference website (http://www.ceradconference.com/) and or by email: cerad@bouesti.edu.ng
