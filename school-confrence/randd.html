research & development forum

Although the University is nascent, the Centre for Research and Development (CERAD) of Bamidele Olumilua University of Education, Science and Technology (BOUESTI) has great scholars who are working assiduously in the areas of education, science and technology. Since its establishment in 2020, the Centre has processed and managed institution based research (IBR) grant from TETFund. Also, a scholar won a national reserve fund (NRF) grant from TETFund.
